What do we do?

Our project is aimed at improving the accuracy of fraud detection in banking transactions by using unsupervised ML/AI programs.

  • Learn More

Why this project?

One of our team members recently (late August) received a strange charge to their bank account for $40 from a domain hosting website in Singapore, supposedly famous for scamming. It was marked as suspicious by the bank and the transaction never went through, but this got us thinking: how much fraud slips through the cracks? Thus, our mission statement: improving fraud detection through a more comprehensive algorithm.
